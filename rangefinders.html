Laser Rangefinder Buying Guide

Laser rangefinders measure distance by emitting a laser pulse and calculating the time it takes to reflect back from a target.

Key Features

  • Maximum measuring distance
  • Accuracy (±1 yard/meter typical)
  • Angle compensation
  • Target priority modes

Applications

Rangefinders are used in hunting, golf, forestry, construction, and surveying. Modern devices are compact, waterproof, and lightweight.

Choosing the Right Model

Select based on intended distance range and environmental conditions. Golf models differ from long-range hunting models.

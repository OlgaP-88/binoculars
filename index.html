Why Optical Knowledge Matters

Many people focus only on magnification numbers, but true optical performance depends on several factors — light transmission, lens quality, prism design, coatings, and mechanical stability. A well-balanced optical device often performs better than one with extreme specifications on paper.

Understanding these factors helps you avoid marketing traps and choose equipment that matches your real needs.

Understanding Optical Microscopes

Microscopes magnify tiny objects invisible to the naked eye. They are essential tools in biology, medicine, research, and education.

Main Types

  • Compound microscopes – high magnification for cells and microorganisms
  • Stereo microscopes – 3D viewing of larger specimens
  • Digital microscopes – display image on screen

Magnification vs Resolution

Higher magnification does not always mean better clarity. Optical quality and lens design determine resolution.

Applications

  • Education
  • Laboratory research
  • Electronics inspection
  • Hobby use

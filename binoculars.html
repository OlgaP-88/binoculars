Understanding Binoculars

Binoculars are portable optical instruments designed for magnifying distant objects. They consist of two aligned telescopes, allowing comfortable two-eye viewing.

Key Specifications

  • Magnification (8x, 10x, 12x)
  • Objective lens diameter (32mm, 42mm, 50mm)
  • Field of view
  • Lens coatings
